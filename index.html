Butter Fun Facts

Butter has been around for thousands of years. The earliest evidence of butter-making dates back to around 2000 BCE in ancient Mesopotamia.

In some cultures, butter was once used as a form of currency. In ancient China and India, butter was so valuable that it was used in trade and even offered as tribute to rulers.

Butter comes in a surprising range of flavors. In addition to traditional salted and unsalted varieties, you can find specialty butters infused with herbs, spices, truffles, or even fruit.
